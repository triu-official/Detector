Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data Detector collects and how it is used when you scan a URL using this service.

What we store

  • Submitted URLs — every URL you submit is stored in a database along with its risk score, label, and detection reasons.
  • Request logs — HTTP method, path, status code, and response time are logged per request for operational monitoring. These are automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Feedback — if you flag a result as incorrect, the analysis ID and timestamp are stored.
  • Admin actions — blacklist additions, exports, and batch scans are logged for audit purposes.

What we do not store

  • We do not store cookies beyond the session cookie required for the admin panel.
  • We do not store your IP address in the analysis records.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Caching

Analysis results may be cached in Redis for up to 30 minutes to avoid re-scanning identical URLs. Cached data is not shared between users.

Your rights

If you wish to request deletion of data associated with a URL you submitted, please contact the administrator of this deployment.
